B2B Lead Generation

The Challenge

Many organisations struggle to consistently reach the right decision-makers. Sales efforts are often unfocused, pipelines are weak, and growth becomes unpredictable.

Our Approach

Pathedge designs and executes structured B2B lead generation programmes. We identify target sectors and organisations, map decision-makers, qualify prospects, and support engagement.

Outcomes

  • Stronger and more predictable sales pipelines
  • Improved lead quality and relevance
  • Better visibility of opportunities

Dormant Account Activation

The Challenge

Inactive customers and institutions represent lost revenue and weakened relationships. Many organisations lack the time or structure to re-engage these accounts effectively.

Our Approach

Pathedge analyses dormant accounts, prioritises re-engagement opportunities, and supports structured communication with key stakeholders.

Outcomes

  • Reactivated customer relationships
  • Recovered revenue opportunities
  • Stronger long-term client retention

Sales Execution & Pipeline Management

The Challenge

Our Approach

Tender Management & Bid Support

Our Approach
