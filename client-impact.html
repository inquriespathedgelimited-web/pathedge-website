Client Impact & Case Highlights

Pathedge Investment Limited partners with organizations across multiple industries to strengthen business development execution, expand market reach, improve sales performance, and deliver sustainable commercial growth.

The anonymised case highlights below demonstrate our structured, execution-focused approach and the measurable impact delivered to our clients.

Technology Hardware Supply Company

Service: B2B Lead Generation & Pipeline Development

Challenge
A technology hardware supply company faced difficulties identifying and accessing qualified B2B clients. Sales efforts were largely opportunistic, resulting in an inconsistent pipeline and limited engagement with decision-makers.

Pathedge Intervention
Pathedge designed and executed a structured B2B lead generation strategy, including market segmentation, decision-maker targeting, disciplined qualification, and pipeline development.

Impact
The client achieved a stronger and more predictable sales pipeline, expanded its customer base, and recorded sustained revenue growth driven by improved lead quality and execution discipline.

Automotive & Heavy Equipment Windscreen Solutions Provider

Service: B2B Market Expansion & Sales Enablement

Challenge
A company specialising in the supply and installation of windscreens for mining equipment, agricultural machinery, heavy trucks, and general vehicles sought to expand its reach across multiple sectors without a structured B2B growth approach.

Pathedge Intervention
Pathedge supported the business through targeted B2B outreach and structured engagement with corporate clients across mining, agriculture, logistics, and fleet operations.

Impact
The client broadened its customer base across multiple industries, strengthened its market presence, and improved the consistency of commercial opportunities.

General Supply Company

Service: Tender Management & Bid Support

Challenge
A general supply company struggled with identifying suitable tender opportunities and preparing compliant, competitive submissions across government and private-sector procurement platforms.

Pathedge Intervention
Pathedge was engaged to manage the tender lifecycle, including tender sourcing, bid/no-bid assessment, compliance review, proposal structuring, and submission coordination.

Impact
The client improved its tender participation and successfully secured multiple tenders across both government and private-sector institutions, strengthening its revenue pipeline and market credibility.

Automotive Spares & Parts Company

Service: B2B Lead Generation & Sales Execution

Challenge
An automotive spares and parts supplier faced slow B2B sales cycles, limited pipeline visibility, and inconsistent follow-up resulting in missed opportunities.

Pathedge Intervention
Pathedge supported the business through structured B2B lead generation combined with hands-on sales execution support, focusing on pipeline discipline, follow-up consistency, and opportunity conversion.

Impact
The client recorded significant improvement in its ready sales pipeline, increased deal flow, and stronger sales performance driven by improved execution and accountability.
