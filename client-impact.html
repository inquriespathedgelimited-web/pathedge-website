Technology Hardware Supply Company

The Challenge

The client operated in the technology hardware supply space and faced challenges building a consistent pipeline of qualified B2B clients. Sales efforts were largely reactive, with limited visibility of decision-makers and opportunities.

Pathedge Intervention

Pathedge implemented structured B2B lead generation and supported sales execution. This included identifying target organisations, mapping decision-makers, qualifying prospects, and improving follow-up discipline.

Outcome

The client recorded improved pipeline visibility, increased qualified opportunities, growth in customer base, and improved revenue performance.
