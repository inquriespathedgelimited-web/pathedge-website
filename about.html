Who We Are

Pathedge Investment Limited is a Zambian Business Development Company focused on helping organisations translate strategy into measurable commercial results.

We work with organisations across multiple industries that require structured market engagement, disciplined sales execution, and professional tender management support.

What We Do

Pathedge specialises in execution-focused services including:

  • B2B Lead Generation and Opportunity Qualification
  • Dormant Account Activation and Relationship Re-engagement
  • Sales Execution and Pipeline Management
  • Tender Management and Bid Support

Our engagements are practical and hands-on. We do not stop at advice — we work alongside client teams to ensure implementation, follow-through, and performance improvement.

Our Approach

We believe sustainable growth is achieved through disciplined execution. Our approach is built around:

  • Designing structured execution plans
  • Supporting implementation on the ground
  • Monitoring progress and refining actions
