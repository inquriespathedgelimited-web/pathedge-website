About Pathedge Investment Limited

A professional business development and commercial growth advisory firm supporting organisations across Zambia and Africa.

Who We Are

Pathedge Investment Limited is a Zambian business development and growth advisory firm established to support organisations in achieving structured, sustainable, and professional commercial growth.

We operate as an extension of our clients’ commercial departments, providing execution capability across business development, sales execution, market expansion, strategic partnerships, tender positioning, and growth strategy.

Our approach is practical, execution-focused, and aligned with real business objectives rather than theoretical consultancy.

Our Approach

We work closely with internal leadership and commercial teams to understand organisational goals, market realities, and execution gaps.

Our engagements are designed to integrate seamlessly with existing structures, ensuring accountability, measurable outcomes, and long-term value creation.

Our Mission

To enable organisations to grow professionally through disciplined business development execution, ethical engagement, and long-term commercial partnerships.

Our Vision

To be a trusted regional partner for organisations seeking structured growth, market access, and commercial execution across Africa.

Professional Accreditation

Pathedge Investment Limited is a registered Corporate Member of the Zambia Institute of Marketing (ZIM), operating in accordance with the Zambia Institute of Marketing Act No. 2 of 2022.

This accreditation reinforces our commitment to professional standards, ethical business development practices, and disciplined commercial execution.
