Who We Are

Pathedge Investment Limited is a Zambian Business Development Company established to support organisations that are ready to grow but face challenges in execution, market access, and conversion. We work with businesses operating across different industries that require structured, disciplined, and results-driven business development support.

We understand the Zambian business environment — from relationship-driven sales and institutional procurement processes to compliance requirements and long sales cycles. This local understanding allows us to design practical solutions that work within the realities of the market.

Our Purpose

Many organisations have strong products, capable teams, and clear ambitions, yet struggle to convert opportunities into results. Common challenges include weak B2B pipelines, dormant customers, stalled sales opportunities, and low success rates in tenders.

Pathedge exists to close this execution gap. Our role is to support organisations by bringing structure, discipline, accountability, and focused execution to their business development efforts.

What We Specialise In

Pathedge focuses on four core areas that directly impact growth and revenue performance:

  • B2B Lead Generation – identifying, qualifying, and engaging decision-makers to build strong and predictable pipelines
  • Dormant Account Activation – re-engaging inactive customers and restoring commercial relationships
  • Sales Execution – strengthening follow-up, pipeline management, and opportunity conversion
  • Tender Management – supporting tender identification, compliance, documentation, and submission

How We Work

Pathedge does not operate as an advisory firm that delivers reports and disengages. We work as an execution partner, supporting our clients on the ground.

Our engagements are structured, practical, and outcome-focused. We work closely with client teams to ensure strategies are implemented, opportunities are followed through, and performance is continuously improved.

Credibility & Professional Standing

Pathedge Investment Limited is a registered Corporate Member of the Zambia Institute of Marketing (ZIM). This reflects our commitment to professional standards, ethical business practices, and disciplined marketing and business development execution.

Who We Work With

We work with SMEs, corporates, suppliers, contractors, financial institutions, NGOs, and organisations that operate within both the private and public sectors. Our clients value structure, accountability, and measurable commercial outcomes.
