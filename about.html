Who We Are

Pathedge Investment Limited is a Zambian Business Development Company established to bridge the gap between strategy and execution. We support organisations that have strong products, services, or opportunities but require structured market access, disciplined sales execution, and professional tender management to achieve measurable commercial results.

Our role goes beyond advisory. Pathedge works as an execution partner, embedded alongside client teams to drive growth, improve performance, and unlock revenue opportunities across both private and public sector markets.

Why Pathedge Exists

Many organisations struggle not because of poor ideas, but due to weak execution, inconsistent follow-up, limited market intelligence, or lack of internal capacity. Pathedge was created to solve this execution gap by providing hands-on business development support that is structured, accountable, and results-driven.

What We Do

  • Build and manage qualified B2B sales pipelines
  • Reactivate dormant customers and institutional accounts
  • Strengthen sales execution and opportunity conversion
  • Support tender identification, preparation, and submission
  • Position organisations for institutional and large-scale opportunities

How We Work

Pathedge applies a disciplined, execution-focused approach:

  • Understanding client objectives and operating context
  • Identifying commercial opportunities and constraints
  • Designing structured execution plans
  • Supporting implementation and follow-up
  • Tracking performance and refining actions

Credibility & Professional Standing

Pathedge Investment Limited is a registered Corporate Member of the Zambia Institute of Marketing (ZIM), reflecting our commitment to professional standards, ethical practice, and disciplined business development.

Who We Work With

We work with SMEs, corporates, financial institutions, construction firms, suppliers, NGOs, and public sector-facing organisations that require structured market engagement and execution support.
