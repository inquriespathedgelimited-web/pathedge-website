Who We Are

Pathedge Investment Limited is a Zambian Business Development Company established to help organisations move from opportunity to execution. We work with businesses that require structured market engagement, disciplined sales execution, and professional tender management to achieve sustainable growth.

Our strength lies in execution. We do not stop at strategy — we work alongside our clients to implement, follow through, and deliver results.

Our Role

Pathedge acts as a business development partner, supporting internal teams where capacity, structure, or market access is limited. We bring discipline, accountability, and commercial focus.

Professional Standing

Pathedge Investment Limited is a registered Corporate Member of the Zambia Institute of Marketing (ZIM), reflecting our commitment to professional standards and ethical practice.
