Dormant Account Activation

Most dormant customers are not lost — they simply need attention, solutions, and improved convenience. Pathedge Investment Limited specializes in re-engaging inactive customers through structured, empathetic, and results-driven engagement.

The Challenge

  • Inactive customer accounts and declining recurring revenue
  • Poor customer follow-up and low engagement levels
  • Unresolved onboarding, service, or communication issues
  • High cost of acquiring new customers to replace existing ones

Our Solution

Pathedge applies a human-centered activation model focused on rebuilding trust, resolving pain points, and restoring value through professional, structured conversations.

Our Activation Process

  • Account Segmentation: Identify why accounts became inactive and categorize them by value and recovery potential
  • Human-Centered Conversations: Empathetic engagement conducted by trained professionals
  • Pain-Point Resolution: Address service delays, onboarding challenges, or communication gaps
  • Reactivation Pathways: Digital onboarding, service reviews, convenience options, or personalized support
  • Activity Monitoring: Track customer behaviour over a 30–90 day period to ensure sustained usage

Outcome

Revived revenue, restored customer confidence, improved retention, and stronger long-term engagement.
