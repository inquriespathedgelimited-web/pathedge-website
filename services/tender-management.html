Tender Management & Bid Support

Tender opportunities represent significant revenue potential, but many organizations struggle due to weak compliance, poor documentation, and limited internal capacity. Pathedge Investment Limited provides end-to-end tender management and bid support services that improve participation, submission quality, and tender win rates.

The Challenge

  • Missed tender opportunities due to lack of visibility
  • Low compliance with tender and procurement requirements
  • Weak proposal structure, content, and presentation
  • Limited internal time and bid management capacity
  • Repeated tender failures without feedback or improvement

Our Solution

Pathedge Investment Limited manages the full tender lifecycle, ensuring accuracy, compliance, competitiveness, and timely submissions across public, donor-funded, and private-sector tenders.

Our Tender Support Services

  • Daily Tender Sourcing: Monitoring opportunities from ZPPA, e-GP, donor platforms, NGOs, and private-sector sources.
  • Bid / No-Bid Assessment: Evaluating relevance, organizational capacity, competitiveness, and compliance requirements.
  • Proposal Preparation: Developing professional, fully compliant technical and commercial bid documentation.
  • Submission Management: Coordinating timelines, documentation, and final submissions to ensure accuracy and completeness.
  • Post-Evaluation Review: Analysing tender outcomes, identifying gaps, and strengthening future submissions.

Outcome

Higher tender participation, stronger and more compliant submissions, improved evaluation scores, and increased tender win ratios.
